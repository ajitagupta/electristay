ElectriStay Showcases EV Charging Solutions at START Summit 2025

March 22, 2025
St. Gallen, Switzerland
5 min read

ElectriStay representatives attended the prestigious START Summit in St. Gallen on March 20-21, connecting with leading software companies, investors, and industry innovators in the electric mobility space.

Event Highlights

The START Summit, one of Europe's premier entrepreneurship conferences, provided an exceptional platform for ElectriStay to showcase our latest advancements in EV charging technology and hospitality integration. The spacious venue hosted numerous booths from fancy AI companies and eager investors looking for the next big opportunity in sustainable transportation.

Networking Opportunities

The well-organized event facilitated meaningful conversations with established investor firms actively seeking promising ventures in the sustainable mobility sector. These connections will help us expand our network in the areas of startups, mobility, and sustainability.

Key topics discussed during the summit included:

  • AI-powered charging optimization algorithms
  • Integration of renewable energy sources with EV charging infrastructure
  • Strategic partnerships with hospitality providers across Europe
  • Scalable solutions for the growing EV tourism market

Looking Ahead

The summit has reinforced our commitment to integrating cutting-edge charging solutions with premium hospitality experiences. The energy and innovation on display have inspired new directions for our upcoming technology releases and property developments.

ElectriStay continues to position itself at the intersection of luxury accommodation and advanced EV infrastructure, with several partnership announcements expected in the coming months as a direct result of connections made at the START Summit.

Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to expand our network and bring innovative charging solutions to premium accommodations across Europe.
