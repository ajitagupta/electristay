Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about joining the ElectriStay network

What are the costs involved?
ElectriStay operates on a commission-based model. There are no upfront fees to join our network. We only earn when you do, taking a small percentage of bookings made through our platform.
Do I need to have charging stations already installed?
No, you don't need existing charging infrastructure. We work with properties at all stages - from those with established charging networks to those just beginning their EV journey. We can help you plan and implement the right charging solution for your property.
What support does ElectriStay provide?
We provide comprehensive support including charging station recommendations, installation partner connections, technical support, marketing your property to EV travelers, staff training materials, and 24/7 customer service for both you and your guests.
How long does the approval process take?
Our team typically reviews applications within 48-72 hours. Once approved, the full onboarding process, including property setup and training, usually takes 1-2 weeks depending on your readiness and charging infrastructure status.
